1. Cleaning Steps Applied

  1. Normalized inconsistent text casing in region (e.g. "north", "NORTH" → "North").
  2. Removed 80 duplicate orders by order_id.
  3. Filled missing unit_price and customer_age with column median; region with mode.
  4. Replaced impossible ages (<14 or >95) with the median age.
  5. Capped 78 price outliers using the IQR rule (bounds $-44.38 – $188.07).
  6. Engineered revenue = units × unit_price × (1 − discount).
